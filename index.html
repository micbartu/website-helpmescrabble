Instructions

  • 1. Put all 7 of your letters into the 7 empty boxes on the screen. Don’t worry about the order.
  • 2. If you want to create a word with a letter already played (which is already on the board) put the letter in the ‘Letter on the board’ box.
  • 3. If there is any particular place in your word you would like the played letter to appear, place the number in the ‘Position of board letter’ box.
  • 4. Press ‘Search’ and scroll down to find all matching words you can play. The further you scroll the longer words can be found.
  • 5. Click on the word you want to place on the board. This will clear the letters you played from the rack.
  • 6. Add the letters you drew from the bag and repeat searching!
